Project WORK

Project WORK (Winning = Opportunities, Resources, and Knowledge) is a transformative workforce development initiative of the Bobby Byars Foundation designed to equip individuals with the tools they need to achieve sustainable employment and long-term success.

The program connects participants to meaningful career opportunities while providing access to essential resources, skills training, mentorship, and professional development. Through education, industry partnerships, and community support, Project WORK empowers individuals to build confidence, increase earning potential, and create pathways toward economic mobility. By focusing on practical knowledge, real-world experience, and personal growth, Project WORK turns opportunity into achievement and potential into progress.

For students interested in STEM fields: Project WORK is preparing a specialized STEM Program. Please let us know if you would like to be added to the waitlist for updates on the STEM Program launch and related opportunities.

Who Is Eligible

Project WORK is open to students in middle school and high school who are interested in making an impact in their community while developing important skills.
