Youth Mentorship Program

Our Youth Mentorship Program pairs at-risk young people with caring, trained adult mentors who guide them through life's challenges. Together, they build trust, develop skills, and work toward a brighter future — one connection at a time.

Since our founding, this program has served hundreds of youth in our community, fostering confidence, academic growth, and lasting relationships that extend well beyond the program itself.

She sat with me through every hard moment, celebrated every small win, and never gave up on me — even when I wanted to give up on myself. Who Is Eligible

This program is open to youth between the ages of 12 and 18 who are experiencing academic, social, or family challenges. Residents of our three-county service area with a demonstrated need for additional support are encouraged to apply.

How to Join

Families can apply by completing a short intake form available on our website or by visiting our office during regular business hours. Once your application is received, a program coordinator will reach out within five business days to schedule an introductory meeting and begin the matching process.
