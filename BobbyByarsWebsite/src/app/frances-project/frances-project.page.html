In Her Honor

The Frances Byars Project

Special projects in memory of Frances Parker Byars that support women and families in crisis. This includes but is not limited to blankets for babies, bereavement support, holiday gifts, and more.

The Frances Project is a compassionate outreach initiative of the Bobby Byars Foundation, created in loving memory of Frances Byars. This program reflects her legacy of service, strength, and commitment to family by providing meaningful support to families experiencing crisis.

The Frances Project serves as the Foundation's primary vehicle for responding to urgent community needs—offering essential resources, guidance, and hands-on assistance while engaging in active community service. Through coordinated support efforts, partnerships, and volunteer-driven initiatives, the program helps stabilize families during difficult times and restores hope where it is needed most.

By honoring Frances Byars' memory through action, The Frances Project embodies the Foundation's commitment to compassion, dignity, and community care.
